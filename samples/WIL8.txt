11. I DECLARE AND DIRECT that: 
 

(a) all income of my estate shall be treated as arising at the time when it is paid 
and shall not be apportioned to any other time or period; 

 
(b) bonus shares issued by a company in which any part of my estate may be 

invested form part of the capital of my estate and are not to be treated as 
income; 

 
(c) where any interest of any person under this Will is subject to that person 

attaining a certain age or living to a certain point of time then such an interest 
shall until it vests in possession be a contingent interest only and shall not be 
deemed to be an interest which is vested subject to any defeasance; 

 
(d) where capital gains tax is assessed against my Trustees either consequent upon 

my death or upon the sale of any asset of my estate by my Trustees then 
(notwithstanding any other provision of this Will charging the residue of my 
estate with the payment of taxes) such tax shall be a charge firstly upon the 
asset giving rise to the tax or upon the net proceeds of sale thereof (as the case 
may be) and secondly, upon my residuary estate PROVIDED ALWAYS that 
where an asset forming part of my residuary estate is disposed of or deemed to 
be disposed of by my Trustees my Trustees shall have the power to determine 
from which part or parts of the capital or income of my residuary estate my 
Trustees will pay any income or capital gains tax liability flowing from the 
disposal or deemed disposal;
